Cookies: We use session cookies only for keeping you logged in during your visit. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.


Third Parties: MediBot does not share your data with any third parties. We do not use analytics tools that track individual users.


Medical Data: Your symptom queries are processed locally by the AI model running on our server. They are not logged or associated with your identity.
